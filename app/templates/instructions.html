CoReBIN Comparison and Evaluation of metagenomic binning

  • Info
  • Instructions

Info

CoReBIN is a user-friendly web-based tool to compare the results of different binning methods and to aid the refinement of the bins. The overall binning result of two different methods can be visually compared in a chord diagram. Individual bins can be inspected using GC%, coverage and tetranucleotide frequencies.
